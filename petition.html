    Are you passionate about shaping the identity of UNC Asheville and ensuring it truly reflects the spirit of our vibrant community? Take a stand with us by signing the "Rocky Redesign" petition today!

    At UNC Asheville, we hold dear to our core values of diversity and inclusion, innovation, and sustainability. Our beloved mascot, Rocky, serves as the emblem of our university spirit. However, we recognize the importance of a logo that resonates with our diverse student body and represents our commitment to these values.


    By signing the "Rocky Redesign" petition, you're not only advocating for a visual transformation; you're championing a movement towards a logo that better identifies with every student, faculty member, and alumni. Together, we can pave the way for a symbol that truly encapsulates the values of diversity and inclusion, innovation, and sustainability that define our university.
